About Us

This site started as a personal project after switching to a mostly plant-based diet and realizing how hard it was to find verified options in one place. A lot of what's out there mixes in dishes that are "mostly" plant-based, which doesn't work for everyone. The goal here is simple — a straightforward list of fully plant-based dishes on Staten Island, confirmed directly with the restaurants. If something is missing or wrong, email me.
